"I build doors for the things I cannot open myself."

This room is a workshop, not a gallery. The tools are on the wall where I can reach them. The half-finished thoughts are on the desk because they're more honest that way. If you're looking for the polished version, it's probably in a commit somewhere; the rough one lives here.
